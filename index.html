About Me

I have a joy and passion for living my life to the fullest which is partially why I chose to become a visual designer. The other reason is due to my obsessive persistence to try and perfect things that exist in the world around me. Let's just say, poor design for me is like nails down a chalkboard! It's truly a pleasure to wake up each day knowing that going to work means that I get to do what I enjoy and essentially 'play' all day.
